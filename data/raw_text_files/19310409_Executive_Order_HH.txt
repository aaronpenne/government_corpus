THE HONORABLE NICHOLAS LONGWORTH
AS A MARK of respect to the memory of the Honorable Nicholas Longworth, late Speaker and Member of the House of Representatives of the United States, it is hereby ordered that the flags on the White House and public buildings in the District of Columbia and the State of Ohio be placed at half mast today and on the day of the funeral service at Cincinnati, Ohio, Saturday, April 11, 1931.
HERBERT HOOVER
The White House,
April 9, 1931.
