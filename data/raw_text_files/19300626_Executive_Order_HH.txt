It is hereby ordered under authority contained in section 5 of the act of February 8, 1887 (24 Stat. 389-390), that the trust period on the allotments of the following named members of the Red Lake Chippewa Tribe of Indians of Minnesota, on the ceded part of the Red Lake Reservation, be, and is hereby, extended for a period of 10 years from the date of expiration. 
ALLOTMENT NUMBER NAME OF ALLOTTEE 
1 Nay-may-pack
2 Ka-ka Keese or Little Crow 3 Ka-Kee-Ka Kee Sick or Everlasting Sky
 HERBERT HOOVER
The White House,
June 26, 1930.
