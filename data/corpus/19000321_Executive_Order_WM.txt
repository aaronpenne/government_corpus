The Secretary of the Navy is hereby directed to transfer to the Secretary of War for use in the transport service of the War Department the vessels
 Badger
 and
 Resolute
 , purchased by the Navy Department from the funds allotted from the emergency appropriation, national defense, act of March 8, 1898, at a cost of $842,000, these vessels being no longer required in the service of the navy.
WILLIAM MCKINLEY
