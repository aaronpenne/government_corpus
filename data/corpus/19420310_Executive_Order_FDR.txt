By virtue of the authority vested in me as President of the United States, and in order to further coordinate and promote the improvement of the surveying and mapping activities of the Government, it is hereby ordered as follows:
1. The Federal Board of Surveys and Maps, established by Executive Order No. 3206 of December 30, 1919, is hereby abolished.
2. The Director of the Office of Management and Budget is authorized and directed to perform the functions heretofore performed by the said Board, and such other functions as may be necessary to carry out the purposes of this order.
[Title and para. 2 amended by EO 12608 of Sept. 9, 1987, 52 FR 34617, 3 CFR, 1987 Comp., p. 245]
3. The Director of the Office of Management and Budget is authorized and directed to call upon the heads of the several departments and independent establishments to furnish, through such representatives as they may designate for the purpose, such information as may be required by the Director in carrying out the provisions of this order.
[Para. 3 amended by EO 12608 of Sept. 9, 1987, 52 FR 34617, 3 CFR, 1987 Comp., p. 245]
