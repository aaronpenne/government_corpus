EXECUTIVE ORDER 8519
RESERVING CERTAIN TOWNSITE LOTS FOR THE USE OF THE FOREST SERVICE
August 16, 1940
WYOMING
By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by the act of June 25, 1910, c. 421, 36 Stat. 847, as amended by the act of August 24, 1912, c. 369, 37 Stat. 497, and subject to valid existing rights, it is ordered that the following-described townsite lots are hereby reserved and set apart for the use of the Forest Service, Department of Agriculture, for administrative purposes:
ESTERBROOK TOWNSITE
Block 1, lots 1 to 5, inclusive,
Block 2, lots 1 to 5, inclusive lots 16 to 20, inclusive,
Block 3, lots 1 to 5, inclusive, lots 16 to 20, inclusive.
FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT
THE WHITE HOUSE,
August 16, 1940.
Exec. Order No. 8519, 5 FR 2882, 1940 WL 3643 (Pres.)
