It is hereby ordered that the S. 1/2 of the SE. 1/4 and the NE. 1/4 of the SW. 1/4 of Section 25, Township 30 North of Range 58 East of the Mount Diablo Meridian, in the State of Nevada, be, and they are hereby, reserved from settlement, entry, sale or other disposition, and set aside for Indian allotment purposes, subject to any prior valid existing rights of any persons thereto. 
WM H TAFT
The White House,
September 16, 1912
