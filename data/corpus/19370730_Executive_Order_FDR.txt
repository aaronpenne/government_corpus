EXECUTIVE ORDER 7679
AMENDMENT OF SUBDIVISION II, SCHEDULE A OF THE CIVIL SERVICE RULES
July 30, 1937
By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by paragraph Eighth, subdivision SECOND, section 2 of the Civil Service Act (22 Stat. 404), it is ordered that Subdivision II of Schedule A of the Civil Service Rules be, and it is hereby, amended by adding thereto the following paragraph:
'4. Chief and two assistant chiefs of the Foreign Service Buildings Office.'
FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT
THE WHITE HOUSE,
July 30, 1937.
Exec. Order No. 7679, 2 FR 1621, 1937 WL 2831 (Pres.)
