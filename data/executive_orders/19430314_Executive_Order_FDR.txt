EXECUTIVE ORDER 9314
REVOKING EXECUTIVE ORDER NO. 8955 OF DECEMBER 1, 1941, EXTENDING THE CLASSIFICATION ACT OF 1923, AS AMENDED, TO CERTAIN POSITIONS IN THE WAR DEPARTMENT AND IN THE NAVY DEPARTMENT AND ESTABLISHING A SALARY DIFFERENTIAL THEREFOR
March 15, 1943
By virtue of the authority vested in me by Title II of the act of November 26, 1940, 54 Stat. 1212, and as President of the United States, and upon recommendation of the Civil Service Commission, and finding that such action is necessary to the more efficient operation of the Government, it is ordered as follows:
1. Executive Order No. 8955 of December 1, 1941, extending the Classification Act of 1923, as amended, to certain positions in the War Department and in the Navy Department and establishing a salary differential therefor, is hereby revoked.
2. This order shall take effect as of March 15, 1943.
FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT
THE WHITE HOUSE,
March 15, 1943.
Exec. Order No. 9314, 8 FR 3279, 1943 WL 4069 (Pres.)
