EXECUTIVE ORDER 7994
MODIFICATION OF OKEFENOKEE WILDLIFE REFUGE
October 27, 1938
GEORGIA
By virtue of the authority vested in me as President of the United States, it is ordered that the Okefenokee Wildlife Refuge in Georgia, established by Executive Order No. 7593 of March 30, 1937 (2 F.R. 627), be, and it is hereby, modified by excluding therefrom the following described lands:
WARE COUNTY, GEORGIA
8TH DISTRICT
Lots 433, 434, 435, 436, 437, 440, 441, and 442;
9TH DISTRICT
Lots 19 and 20.
FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT
THE WHITE HOUSE,
October 27, 1938.
Exec. Order No. 7994, 3 FR 2581, 1938 WL 3825 (Pres.)
