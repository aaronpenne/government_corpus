Executive order of August 13, 1923 (No. 3893), which transferred to the Secretary of the Interior certain lands for disposition under the act of July 5, 1884 (23 Stat., 103), is hereby modified and rescinded in so far as it concerns Lot 6, Sec, 11, containing 16.31 acres, and Lot 7, Sec. 12, containing 34.05 acres, embraced in the Nee-ah Harbor Abandoned Military Reservation, and the unsurveyed S/2 of Waadah Island Abandoned Military Reservation, said S/2 containing approximately 14 2 acres, all in T. 33 N., R. 15 W., W.M., State of Washington, and the said tracts are hereby placed under the jurisdiction and control of the Treasury Department for coast guard purposes.
CALVIN COOLIDGE
The White House,
January 28, 1924
