By the authority vested in me as President by the Constitution and the law of the United States of America, and in order to increase the membership of the Presidential Advisory Council on HIV/AIDS, it is hereby ordered that Executive Order No. 12963 is amended by deleting the number "30" in the second sentence of section 1(a) of that order and inserting the number "35" in lieu thereof.
William J. Clinton
The White House,
June 14, 1996.
[Filed with the Office of the Federal Register, 8:45 a.m., June 17, 1996]
