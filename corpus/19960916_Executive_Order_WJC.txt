By the authority vested in me as President by the Constitution and the laws of the United States of America, and in order to add 3 members to the National Bioethics Advisory Commission, it is hereby ordered that the number "15" in the second sentence of section 3(a) of Executive Order No. 12975 is deleted and the number "18" is inserted in lieu thereof.
William J. Clinton
The White House,
September 16, 1996.
[Filed with the Office of the Federal Register, 8:45 a.m., September 17, 1996]
