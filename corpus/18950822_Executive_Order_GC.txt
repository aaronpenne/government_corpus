Government Printing Office Rule II, section 2, is hereby amended by omitting in line I, after the words "under 21," the words "or over 45," and in line 2, after the words "under 18," the words "or over 35." The section as amended will read as follows:
2. Any male citizen of the United States not under 21 years of age and any female citizen not under 18 years of age may be examined for positions in the Government Printing Office.
Approved:
GROVER CLEVELAND.
