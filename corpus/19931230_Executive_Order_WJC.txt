By the authority vested in me as President by the Constitution and the laws of the United States of America, and in order to provide for the appointment of up to 30 members to the United States Advisory Council on the National Information Infrastructure, it is hereby ordered that section 1(a) of Executive Order No. 12864 is amended by deleting the number "25" and inserting the number "30" in lieu thereof.
William J. Clinton
The White House,
December 30, 1993.
[Filed with the Office of the Federal Register, 2:48 p.m., January 3, 1994]
