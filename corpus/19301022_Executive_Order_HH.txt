It is hereby ordered, under authority contained in section 5 of the act of February 8, 1887 (24 Stat. 389), that the trust period on allotments made to members of the Cass Lake, Leech Lake, White Oak Point, and Winnibigoshish bands of Chippewa Indians of Minnesota, which trust period expires during the calendar year 1930, be, and his hereby, extended for a further period of 10 years, with the exception of the following:
