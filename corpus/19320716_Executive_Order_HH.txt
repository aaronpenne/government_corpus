Under authority of the act of Congress approved June 25, 1910 (36 Stat. 847-848), as amended by the act of August 24, 1912 (37 Stat. 497), it is hereby ordered that the following-described tracts of public lands in New Mexico be, and they are hereby, withdrawn from settlement, location, sale, or entry, except as provided in said acts, for use in connection with the San Carlos Indian Irrigation Project, subject to any valid rights or claims initiated prior to the date hereof:
