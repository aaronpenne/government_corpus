It is hereby ordered, under authority contained in section 5 of the act of February 8, 1887 (24 Stat. 389), that the trust period on allotments made to members of the Prairie Band of Potawatomi Indians in Kansas, which trust period expires during the calendar year 1930, be, and is hereby, extended for a period of ten years, with the exception of the following:
