EXECUTIVE ORDER 8443
DIRECTING THE SECRETARY OF THE TREASURY TO ASSEMBLE ANNUALLY A COAST GUARD PERSONNEL BOARD
June 14, 1940
By virtue of and pursuant to the authority vested in me by section 2 of the act of January 12, 1938, 52 Stat. 4 (U.S.C., title 14, sec. 175b, Supp. V), as amended by the act of June 6, 1940, Public No. 556, 76th Congress, I hereby authorize and direct the Secretary of the Treasury to assemble annually a Coast Guard Personnel Board, as provided in the said section.
This order supersedes Executive Order No. 7922 of June 30, 1938, directing the Secretary of the Treasury to assemble annually a Coast Guard Personnel Board.
FRANKLIN D. ROOSEVELT
THE WHITE HOUSE,
June 14, 1940.
Exec. Order No. 8443, 1940 WL 3590 (Pres.)
